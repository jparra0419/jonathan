Profile

Education and Awards

Graduated from O'Connor High School,San Antonio,Texas

Scholarship at UTSA to study piano performance.

Graduated from University of Texas at San Antonio-Bachelor Major Statistics and Minor in Mathematics. Later business scholarship for Statistics.

Started piano at 5 years old.

Studied in middle school at Vivace Music Academy in San Antonio where Jonathan received several awards in piano competitions including trophies.He performed a recital at the end of his senior year at O'Connor High School.

In high school Jonathan won a silver medal in an international competition. Winners recital performance at Carnegie Hall in New York City. He played Rachmaninoff,"Prelude in C# Minor".

Jonathan photo at Carnegie Hall

In 2007 Jonathan began music ministry at Our lady of Guadalupe Church in San Antonio until 2018 when he moved to Venice, Florida.
